Shipping Policy

At Ehsan Products, we take pride in our service. To ensure your products arrive safely, please review our shipping guidelines below.

1. Delivery Timeframe

Standard Shipping: All orders are processed and shipped via our courier partners. Orders will be delivered within 5-7 business days to your doorstep.

Processing Time: Orders are typically prepared for dispatch within 24–48 hours of your purchase. You will receive an email/SMS confirmation once your order is on its way.

2. Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight of your order and your delivery location.

We charge a flat rate of ₹60 for shipping all over India.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, we will provide you with a Tracking Number. You can use this to monitor your shipment's progress in real-time.

4. Packaging & Safety

Because our products contain herbal ingredients and are sealed to preserve purity, we use specialized protective packaging to maintain temperature stability and prevent damage during transit.

5. Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is entered correctly. Ehsan Products is not responsible for delayed or undelivered packages due to incorrect or incomplete address information provided at checkout.

6. Damaged Goods

If your package arrives damaged, please take a photo immediately and contact us at support@ehsanproducts.com within 24 hours of delivery. We will prioritize sending you a fresh replacement.

We have 7-days return policy. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 7 days from the date of delivery.
